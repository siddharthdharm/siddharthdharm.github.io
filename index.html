Ernst & Young (EY)
May'18 - Jul'18

I worked with a team to design and develop a 'Consolidation and Analytics Tool' for the company using C# and .NET framework.
The first part consisted of building a portal to accumulate all trial balances, journal and ledger entries and create Consolidated Financial Statements, viz. Balance Sheets and Profit & Loss Statements for the company.
The second bit of the tool was to automate the calculations of Key Performance Indicators for the company (which essentially are certain financial ratios) and perform sensitivity analysis on them.
The final part of the project was to integrate analytics and graphical visualisations into the tool using R.
I worked under Mr. Darshan Verma (Partner), Mr. Gopal Kuchibhotla (Senior Manager) and Mr. Anshuman Ghosh (Vice President).

Tata Communications Ltd.
May'17 - Aug'17

I designed a software which determines the best possible communication lines for the company, from a given dense network of lines connecting cities around the globe. Interesting features such as selection of intermediate nodes (routes), cost-effective routing, and non-congested routing were implemented.
The software was developed using Java and used some heavy concepts from Graph Theory.
I worked under Mr. Prasad Butala.

Mytrah Energy Pvt. Ltd.
Jan'17 - May'17

Designed and implemented a system which calculates power and energy values, performs other complex computations and generates efficiency curves for a particular windmill. This portal would take in raw data input such as weather conditions, geographical co-ordinates, electronic power supply data, etc. and would perform a test as to whether or not a windmill is functioning at its peak performance levels. Moreover, the software would suggest changes to be incorporated in the windmill for it to function at an optimal level.
I worked under Dr. Aruna Malapati - Assistant Professor, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus and Mr. Prasad Gaitonde.

EduSearch Engine
Oct'16 - May'17

Worked in the area of Information Retrieval to design a web search engine using the PageRank Algorithm to return a set of websites containing terms/explanations/codes related to the field of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. A web scraper, built in Python was used to crawl over the web and gather data. The entire code was written in Python (including pre-processing and indexing of data).
This project was completed under Prof. Aruna Malapati.

Surface Plasmonics in Bi-Metallics
Oct'16 - May'17

Worked under Dr. Kannan Ramaswamy (Professor, Dept. of Physics) in the area of Plasmonics, to perform computational analysis on Indium, Gallium, Tin and their alloys in different physical states and temperatures, upon oblique incidence of light.
Augmented the research by identifying new properties of bi-metallic strips made of Gold and Silver, using Theory of Electromagnetism.
Presented two papers at the Surface Plasmon Photonics (SPP8) International Conference in Taiwan, 2017.

Sun Tracking Solar Panel
Jan'17 - Apr'17

Worked under Dr. Soumya J (Assistant Professor, Dept. of Electrical Engineering) Built a working prototype of a solar panel under Prof. Soumya J. which tracks the movement of the sun using Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs), photo diodes, stepper and servo motors in order to maximize the area exposed to sunlight. The prototype showed excellent results with an increased efficiency of around 21% as compared to standard solar panels available in the market.
